Introduction

The military outpost was, in fact, part of the old town, perched above it, standing watch. The walls which encircled the outpost wound their way down to the port on the east side of the city. The narrow streets and colourful buildings gave the small city a vibrancy that had enticed and entertained many travelers.
The sun shone high and dry above the old town, shining directly into the courtyard of the military outpost. There was stillness in the air that was echoed in the courtyard. The papers had announced his firing squad for noon of the twelfth. People moved quietly about, as if breaking the silence would be to acquiesce that James’ sentence was justified.
